This exploratory analysis highlights where gendered pricing differs across product classes and marketplaces. The goal is to identify where consumers, regulators, and platforms can target transparency interventions first.

Focus groups impacted include frequent personal-care buyers, lower-income households exposed to recurring price gaps, and policy actors evaluating discriminatory market patterns.
